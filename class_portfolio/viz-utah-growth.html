Visualizing Utah's Population Growth

My end of semester project visualizes Utah's population growth as demonstrated by the 2020 US Census.

Brief
There are three graphs:
  1. A bar chart showing the fastest versus slowest growing states
  2. A stacked bar chart breaking out migration from aboard and internal to the US for those states
  3. A circle pack showing from which states people move to Utah. This graph can be color coded by either region or division (sub-region).
